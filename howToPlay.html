Fight Instructions

In this game, you will need to fight various enemies to progress. Here's how to fight:

  1. Use the arrow keys to move your character around.
  2. Press the "Z" key to attack with your weapon.
  3. Aim your weapon at the enemy using the arrow keys.
  4. Press the "X" key to use a special attack, if available.
  5. Avoid enemy attacks by dodging with the arrow keys.
  6. Watch your health bar and use health potions if necessary.
  7. Defeat all enemies in the area to progress to the next level.
