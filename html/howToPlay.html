Fight Instructions

Here's how to fight:

    Player 1

  1. "A" "D" and "W" arrow keys to move your character around
  2. "Space" key to attack with your weapon
  3. Aim your weapon at the enemy using the 'A''W''D' keys

    4. Player 2

  4. "Up" "Left" and "Right" arrow keys to move your character
  5. "Down" arrow key to attack with your weapon
  6. Aim your weapon at the enemy using the arrow keys
